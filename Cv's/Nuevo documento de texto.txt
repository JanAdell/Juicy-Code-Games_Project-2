<h2 id="team-leader">Game Designer</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Jan Adell: <a href="https://github.com/JanAdell">JanAdell</a></li>
</ul>
    
    <p>My name is Jan Adell and I was Lead Game Designer in Juicy Code’s Square Up project.
My workload has been divided in two groups mainly, these being Specific Design work,
where the concept for the game was developed and the game balance was made, and
General work, consisting of tasks pertaining to other areas, such as programming or art.

</p>
    
    <p>Specific work:</p>
    
  <li>
    <p>Game Design Document assembly:
</p>
  </li>
<ul>
  <li>
    <p>Game Pillar definition.
</p>
  </li>
  <li>
    <p>Basic idea analysis.
</p>
  </li>
  <li>
    <p>Gameplay features.
</p>
  </li>
  <li>
    <p>Campaign and quest definition.</p>
  </li>
  <li>
    <p>Enemy and ally unit and building definition.</p>
  </li>
  <li>
    <p>Map rough design and gameplay flow.</p>
  </li>
  <li>
    <p>Proofreading and correcting of the Setting and Story documents.</p>
  </li>
  <li>
    <p>Proofreading and correcting of any document pertaining to the design specific
parts of the project’s wiki.</p>
  </li>
</ul>
  <li>
    <p>Game Balance:</p>
	</li>
	<ul>
      <li>
        <p>Unit stat balancing.</p>
      </li>
      <li>
        <p>Building stat balancing.</p>
      </li>
<li>
        <p>Edge, Gears and Gold currency/resource gain tweaking.</p>
      </li>
<li>
        <p>Economy systems utilization.</p>
      </li>
<li>
        <p>Unit and building price definition and balance.</p>
      </li>
   </ul>
